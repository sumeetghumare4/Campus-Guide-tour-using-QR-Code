Vasantrao Pawar Law College

Vasantrao Pawar Law College is an institution established in 1999 by Vidya Pratishthan, a trust registered under Bombay Public Trust Act
and also a society registered under Societies Registration Act. The College is affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University and it is
recognized by the Bar Council of India.Presently the College conducts three years LL.B. course and five years B.A. LL.B. course.
